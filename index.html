Awareness & Tips

How to Help Injured Dogs

Learn the essential first aid steps to help injured stray dogs. Know when to call professionals and how to safely approach injured animals. Always maintain a safe distance initially and look for signs of aggression or fear.

What to Feed Strays

Discover safe and nutritious food options for stray animals. Learn what foods to avoid and how to provide proper nourishment. Remember to always provide fresh water along with food, and maintain regular feeding schedules if possible.

Creating Safe Spaces

Tips on creating temporary shelters and safe spaces for stray animals in your community, especially during extreme weather. Learn about using readily available materials to create weather-resistant shelters.

Emergency Response Guide

Quick guide on what to do when you find an injured or distressed animal. Learn about emergency contacts, temporary care procedures, and how to transport injured animals safely to veterinary facilities.

Community Involvement

Ways to engage your local community in stray animal welfare. Organize feeding programs, create awareness campaigns, and build a network of animal welfare supporters in your neighborhood.

Winter Care Tips

Special considerations for helping stray animals during cold weather. Learn about creating warm shelters, providing appropriate food, and ensuring water doesn't freeze.

Summer Protection

Guidelines for protecting strays during hot weather. Tips include providing shade, ensuring constant water availability, and recognizing signs of heat stress in animals.

Vaccination Awareness

Understanding the importance of vaccinations for stray animals. Learn about common diseases, vaccination schedules, and how to coordinate with local vets for vaccination drives.
